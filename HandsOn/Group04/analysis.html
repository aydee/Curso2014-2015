Analysis of the Data and License

Group04

The system "Search Tourism Madrid" is an application intended mainly for tourism and Madrileños people. This app will help to locate the most famous touristic places and cultural interest. Besides, if the user is looking for a museum, monument, embassy, consulate or building he will be able to use a filter to locate close places, and the application offers numerous possibilities of places to visit in the district or neighbourhood.

Analysis of the License

Madrid Open Data License

The website of the License allows us to use, control, manage and update the data but at the same time gives us some conditions if we want to use its information. Besides, the data are free. These general conditions are the next:
  • We could manage the information, but its prohibited to change the meaning of them
  • We must attribute the source of the documents by the next sentence: "Origen de los datos: Ayuntamiento de Madrid"
  • We must mention the date on which data was last refreshed, if it was written by the "Ayuntamiento de Madrid"
  • We may not indicate or suggest that "Ayuntamiento de Madrid" is participating in the modification of the data
  • We should not delete, change or remove the metadata and the date of the updates and the conditions to use the information
