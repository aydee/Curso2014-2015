0. Description

The context for app is: What do you think when you are going to buy or rent a house ?

  • Neighborhood ?
  • Crime rates around ?
  • Public services ?
  • Transport ?
  • Your work location ?
  • None of the above ?

Our application will help you to decide which neighborhood fits you best.

1. Start application

At the beginning, it will display a search neighborhoods in the city.

The user can enter a neighborhood or an address.

2. Use

Application present neighborhood score when the user search.

This score will be based on an estimate crime rate, nearby schools or public buildings, transport, etc.

Finally, the user can visualize any options for choose his best place.
