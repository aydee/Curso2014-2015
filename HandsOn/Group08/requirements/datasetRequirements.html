0. Datasets

The chosen datasets about San Francisco City are (click to download original):

  • 311 Service Requests
  • Crime
  • GIS Mapping
  • GTFS Public Transport
  • Public Buildings
  • Public and Private Schools

1. Smart Cities Domain

The chosen datasets have some properties in the context of smart cities for this task:

  • Since they promote the cooperation and use of data by the comunity users.
  • To contibute to raise life quality of the users.
  • Each one have geospatial representation for link with real world.
  • Finally, they promote the proposed smart cities requirements in the hands on.

2. Data are available in a CSV file

Some datasets have Shapefile or similar format.

We have used GDAL Library, Google Fusion Tables and Open Refine to convert them to CSV.

3. Data have a open license

The license in all cases is CC0 1.0 Universal. Datasets are available online for free.

4. Data can easily linked with real-wold entities

The link in all cases is GPS coordinates.

For example, Both public and private schools are physical buildings.

5. Documentation exist for the data (optional)

GTFS Public Transport dataset have documentation at Google due to it is Generic format. [Link to Google Documentation]

311 Service Request dataset have documentation because it use 311 Standard. [Link to Open311 Documentation]

Zoning GIS use Planning Department's Map Library Summaries of the Zoning District codes. [Link to GIS Documentation]
