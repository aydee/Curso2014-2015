Application Requirements

  • Aim

    • Users can look for information about sightseeing, and where the tourist offices are located in Tenerife

  • Requirements
    1. Users can retrieve all the tourists sites that can be visited
    2. Users can retrieve the locations of the tourists sites
    3. Users can retrieve the schedules of the tourists sites
    4. Users can retrieve directions to go to the tourist sites
    5. Users can retrieve information about tourist offices, where they are located and schedules
    6. Users can retrieve descriptions of the tourists sites
