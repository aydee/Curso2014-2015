Dataset Requirements

  • Data are in the smart cities domain
    • The dataset selected are related to services and locations from the city of Madrid.
  • Data are available as a CSV file
    • The datasets are all avalaible in csv format.
  • Data have an open license so it can be published
    • The terms of use of this data can be found in the following license: Datos Abiertos Madrid
  • Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities
    • The entities contained in these datasets are from the city of Madrid and therefore can be easily linked with generic real-world entities.
